About Solo Developer

Solo Developer is an independent creator platform focused on helping developers, artists, game creators, students, and innovators share their projects with the world.

The platform provides creator profiles, publishing tools, community engagement systems, media uploads, project discovery, and future creator-focused services.

Platform Highlights

  • Creator Profiles
  • Project Publishing
  • Image & Video Uploads
  • Community Interaction
  • Follower System
  • Creator Dashboard
  • Search & Discovery Tools
  • Cloud-Based Infrastructure

Featured Project

One of the first projects associated with Solo Developer is Run From Epsteins, a psychological horror survival game currently under development.

The project focuses on atmosphere, environmental storytelling, suspense, exploration, and immersive horror experiences.

Mission

Our mission is to make it easier for independent creators to publish their work, build audiences, and showcase projects without requiring large teams or expensive infrastructure.
