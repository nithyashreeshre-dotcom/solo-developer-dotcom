// ACCESSIBILITY STATEMENT

At Solo Developer, we believe technology should be accessible to everyone.

Our goal is to create a platform where creators, developers, artists, gamers, and community members can access content, publish projects, and participate in the platform regardless of their abilities or devices.

1. Our Commitment

We are committed to continuously improving the accessibility of Solo Developer and making the platform easier to navigate, understand, and use.

Accessibility is an ongoing effort, and improvements will continue as the platform evolves.

2. Accessibility Features

Solo Developer aims to support:

  • Responsive layouts for different screen sizes
  • Mobile and desktop compatibility
  • Clear navigation structures
  • Readable typography and spacing
  • Keyboard-accessible navigation where supported
  • Consistent interface design
  • Scalable content and layouts

3. Browser & Device Support

Solo Developer is designed to function across modern browsers and devices, including desktop computers, laptops, tablets, and smartphones.

Performance and appearance may vary depending on device capabilities, browser versions, and accessibility tools being used.

4. Continuous Improvements

Accessibility improvements are reviewed during platform development.

Future enhancements may include:

  • Improved keyboard navigation
  • Enhanced screen reader support
  • Additional contrast and readability options
  • Accessibility-focused interface improvements
  • Improved form usability

5. Creator Content

Content published by users may vary in accessibility depending on how it is created and uploaded.

We encourage creators to provide clear titles, descriptions, and accessible content whenever possible.

6. Feedback & Suggestions

Accessibility is a community effort.

If you encounter accessibility issues, usability challenges, or have suggestions for improvement, we welcome your feedback.

Your input helps make Solo Developer more inclusive and accessible for all users.

7. Ongoing Development

Solo Developer is actively developed and continuously improving.

Accessibility considerations will remain part of future platform updates, creator tools, community features, and user experiences.

// OUR GOAL

Build a platform that is usable, inclusive, and welcoming to as many creators and community members as possible.
