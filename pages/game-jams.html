What is a Game Jam?

A game jam is a time-limited challenge where developers create games based on a specific theme, idea, mechanic, or creative restriction.

Game jams encourage experimentation, learning, teamwork, rapid prototyping, and creative problem-solving.

Future Solo Developer Events

As the community grows, Solo Developer plans to host its own game jams and creator challenges.

These events will be open to developers of all experience levels, from beginners creating their first project to experienced creators building ambitious prototypes.
