This Cookie Policy explains how Solo Developer uses cookies and similar technologies to improve user experience, enhance platform functionality, and maintain account security.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your session, preferences, and interactions.

2. Why We Use Cookies

Solo Developer may use cookies and similar technologies for:

  • User authentication and login sessions
  • Account security and fraud prevention
  • Remembering user preferences
  • Improving website performance
  • Analytics and usage insights
  • Enhancing creator and community experiences

3. Types of Cookies

The platform may use the following categories of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies – Required for core website functionality.
  • Security Cookies – Help protect accounts and platform integrity.
  • Preference Cookies – Store settings and user preferences.
  • Analytics Cookies – Help us understand platform usage and performance.

4. Third-Party Services

Solo Developer utilizes third-party services that may place cookies or similar technologies on your device.

These services may include:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Cloudinary Media Services
  • Hosting and Content Delivery Services

5. Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow users to control, block, or delete cookies through browser settings.

Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality and user experience.

6. Data Protection

Cookies used by Solo Developer are intended to improve platform performance, account security, and user experience.

We do not use cookies to intentionally collect sensitive personal information beyond what is necessary for platform operation.

7. Policy Updates

This Cookie Policy may be updated periodically as the platform evolves, new features are introduced, or legal requirements change.

Continued use of Solo Developer after updates indicates acceptance of the revised Cookie Policy.
