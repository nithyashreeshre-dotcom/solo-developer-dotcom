How it works

From idea to audience in minutes

No gatekeeping. No approval process. You create, you publish, you connect.

1
👤
Create your account
Sign up free in under 30 seconds. No credit card needed. Your creator profile is your home base.
2
⬆️
Upload your creation
Games, apps, videos, photos, posts — upload a file or drop in a link. Files go to Cloudinary for free storage.
3
🌐
Go live instantly
Your project is saved to Firestore and gets its own page the moment you hit publish. No waiting, no review.
4
📈
Grow your audience
Track real downloads and visits via Firebase Analytics. Post devlogs, respond to feedback, build a following.

Why creators choose Solo Developer

Built for people who make things solo

No publisher. No team. Just you, your work, and an audience that cares about indie creation.

🆓
Free forever for creators
Publishing your work on Solo Developer is always free. No listing fees, no upload costs. Optional paid plans add analytics and custom domains.
📊
Real analytics, powered by Firebase
Every click, download, and page view is tracked via Firebase Analytics. Real numbers, no vanity metrics. Know exactly how your work is discovered.
🔒
Secure auth & file storage
Accounts are protected by Firebase Authentication. Files upload directly to Cloudinary — free, fast, and globally distributed with CDN delivery.
🌍
Global reach, zero gatekeeping
No approval process, no review queue. Publish and your work is live to everyone instantly. Reach people in 180+ countries.
📝
Built-in devlog system
Write devlogs, tutorials, and posts stored directly in Firestore. Build an audience while you build your project.
💰
Sell your work, keep more
Set your own price, offer free versions, or "pay what you want." Lower platform fee than any competitor. Direct payouts.
