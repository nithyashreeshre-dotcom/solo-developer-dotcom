Why creators choose Solo Developer

Built for people who make things solo

No publisher. No team. Just you, your work, and an audience that cares about indie creation.

Free forever for creators
Publishing your work on Solo Developer is always free. No listing fees, no upload costs. Optional paid plans add analytics and custom domains.
📊
Real analytics, no vanity metrics
See genuine download counts, page visits, and referrer data. No inflated numbers. Know exactly how your work is being discovered.
🔒
You own your content
Your files, your rights. Solo Developer never claims ownership. You can delete everything, export your data, or move elsewhere at any time.

Global reach, zero gatekeeping
No approval process, no review queue. Publish and your work is live to everyone instantly. Reach people in 180+ countries.
